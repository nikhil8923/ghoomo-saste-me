Best Time to Visit Kedarnath

Plan your spiritual journey wisely!

May - June

Best Season

5°C - 15°C
Clear Weather

Temple opens in May. Pleasant weather, clear skies. Best for helicopter service and trekking. Peak yatra season.

Perfect Time

July - August

Risky Season

3°C - 12°C
Heavy Rainfall

Monsoon brings landslides, trek dangerous. Helicopter service disrupted. Only visit if necessary with proper precautions.

Avoid if Possible

Good Season

0°C - 10°C
Moderate Weather

Post-monsoon clarity. Less crowded than peak season. Cold but manageable. Temple closes early November.

Important Travel Tips

Essential tips for safe yatra!

Health Precautions

  • Get medical fitness certificate if over 60 years
  • Carry altitude sickness medicines (Diamox)
  • Bring oxygen cylinder if needed
  • Stay hydrated - drink plenty of water

Documentation

  • Valid photo ID mandatory (Aadhar/PAN)
  • Keep copies of all documents
  • Registration form for helicopter
  • Emergency contact numbers noted down

What to Pack

  • Heavy woolen clothes - very cold
  • Rain gear and waterproof jacket
  • Comfortable trekking shoes
  • Basic medicines and first-aid kit

Food & Drink

  • Light meals before trek/helicopter
  • Carry dry fruits and energy bars
  • Avoid alcohol completely
  • Vegetarian food preferred in temples

Photography

  • No photos inside main sanctum
  • Respect other devotees' privacy
  • Drone photography prohibited
  • Carry extra batteries - cold drains fast

Safety First

  • Follow guide instructions strictly
  • Don't rush - acclimatize properly
  • Stay with group always
  • Check weather before trek/helicopter
