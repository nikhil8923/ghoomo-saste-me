Founder ki Kahani

Namaste! Main hoon Rajesh Kumar, Ghoomo Saste Me ka founder. Meri kahani shuru hoti hai 2019 mein, jab main aur mere dost ek simple Manali trip plan kar rahe the.

Kaise aaya idea ₹5000 fixed pricing ka?

Trip planning karte waqt humein pata chala ki budget travel packages mein transparency nahi hai. Hidden charges, unclear itineraries, aur last minute surprises - yeh sab bahut common tha. Ek package ₹5000 dikha raha tha, lekin final bill ₹8000-9000 aa gaya!

Main socha - kyun nahi ek aisi company banai jaye jahan price fixed ho, transparent ho, aur middle-class families bhi premium travel experience le sakein? Bas yahi se shuru hua hamara journey.

Challenges & Vision

Starting mein bahut challenges the - hotels trust nahi kar rahe the fixed pricing pe, customers ko believe nahi ho raha tha "no hidden charges" wali baat. But hum committed rahe apne mission pe.

Aaj 1000+ happy travelers ke saath, 12 beautiful destinations, aur industry awards ke saath, hum prove kar chuke hain ki affordable travel without quality compromise possible hai!

"Har Indian ko travel karne ka haq hai. ₹5000 mein premium experience dena humara commitment hai!"

- Rajesh Kumar, Founder & CEO
