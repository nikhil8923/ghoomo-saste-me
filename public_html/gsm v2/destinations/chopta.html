Important Travel Tips

Essential tips for safe trek!

Trek Essentials

  • Trekking shoes mandatory—good grip needed
  • Warm clothes—nights very cold (5-10°C)
  • Rain jacket/windcheater essential
  • Basic medicines and first-aid kit

Communication

  • Limited network—BSNL works sometimes
  • Inform family before trek
  • Carry power bank—charging limited
  • Emergency contact with guide

Fitness & Health

  • Moderate fitness level required
  • Acclimatize properly—take breaks
  • Stay hydrated—carry water bottle
  • Avoid heavy meals before trek

Food & Water

  • Carry energy bars and dry fruits
  • Hot soup available at camp
  • Vegetarian meals at campsite
  • Water purification tablets useful

Photography

  • Sunrise at Chandrashila—best shots
  • Carry extra batteries—cold drains fast
  • Wide-angle lens for landscapes
  • Protect gear from moisture

Safety First

  • Follow guide instructions strictly
  • Trek in group—don't wander alone
  • Weather can change quickly—be prepared
  • Know your limits—don't overexert
