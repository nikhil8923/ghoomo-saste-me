Important Travel Tips

Essential tips for a smooth journey!

Pack Wisely

  • Carry warm clothes even in summer (evenings are cold)
  • Sunscreen, sunglasses, and lip balm mandatory
  • Comfortable walking shoes for trekking
  • Basic medicines and first-aid kit

Stay Connected

  • Limited network in remote areas - inform family
  • Keep offline maps downloaded
  • Carry power banks for charging
  • Note down emergency contact numbers

Transportation

  • Book vehicles in advance during peak season
  • Roads can be narrow and winding - avoid night travel
  • Carry motion sickness tablets
  • Check weather conditions before traveling to Rohtang

Food & Health

  • Stay hydrated - altitude can cause dehydration
  • Avoid heavy meals immediately after arrival
  • Try local Himachali cuisine but eat from clean places
  • Acclimatize properly before adventure activities

Photography

  • Best shots during golden hour (sunrise/sunset)
  • Respect local culture - ask before photographing people
  • Carry extra memory cards and batteries
  • Protect camera gear from snow and moisture

Safety First

  • Always inform someone about your itinerary
  • Don't venture into unknown areas alone
  • Follow guide instructions during adventure activities
  • Keep valuables in hotel safe
