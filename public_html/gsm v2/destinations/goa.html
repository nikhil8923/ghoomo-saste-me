Important Travel Tips

Essential tips for a smooth journey!

Pack Wisely

  • Light cotton clothes - beach wear, shorts, t-shirts
  • Swimsuit aur extra change of clothes
  • Sunscreen (SPF 50+), sunglasses, hat mandatory
  • Flip-flops for beach, comfortable shoes
  • Waterproof phone cover for water activities
  • Power bank, camera aur beach bag

Health & Safety

  • Stay hydrated - heat aur alcohol se dehydration
  • Seafood allergy check karo pehle
  • Avoid swimming during monsoon - dangerous
  • Lifeguard wali beaches choose karo
  • Travel insurance with water sports coverage
  • Basic first aid kit carry karo

Money & Transport

  • Rent scooters (₹300-500/day) for easy mobility
  • ATMs easily available - no cash shortage
  • Daily budget: ₹800-1200 for meals & drinks
  • Water sports: ₹3000-5000 extra budget
  • Bargaining works at flea markets
  • UPI widely accepted at most places

Local Insights

  • Beach shacks have best seafood - must try!
  • Respect beach cleanliness - no littering
  • Wednesday: Anjuna market, Saturday: Night bazaar
  • Drinking alcohol on beaches illegal - police fines
  • Book water sports through hotels - avoid touts
  • Try Goan fish curry, prawn balchao, bebinca!

Photography Tips

  • Best shots during golden hour (sunrise/sunset)
  • Colorful Portuguese houses in Panjim - Instagram!
  • Fort Aguada lighthouse sunset view perfect
  • Beach shacks aur flea markets colorful
  • Carry extra memory cards and batteries
  • Protect camera gear from sand and water

Nightlife Safety

  • Stay in groups during late night parties
  • Keep valuables in hotel safe
  • Don't accept drinks from strangers
  • Book cabs through hotel for safe return
  • Know your alcohol limits - stay safe
  • Emergency numbers saved in phone
