Important Travel Tips

Trek safely and smartly!

Safety First

  • Start trek early morning
  • Follow guide instructions always
  • Stay hydrated throughout
  • Don't rush - maintain steady pace

What to Carry

  • Good trekking shoes mandatory
  • Warm clothes and rain jacket
  • Water bottle and snacks
  • Torch, power bank, basic medicines

Photography

  • Sunrise and sunset best times
  • Waterproof cover for camera
  • Extra batteries important
  • Respect nature - no littering

Food & Health

  • Light meals before trek
  • Carry energy bars and chocolates
  • Altitude sickness - watch for symptoms
  • No alcohol during trek
